Robert Latus

I’m a programmer, photographer, and adventurer who’s passionate about building technology that makes a difference. With a background in Information Technology and over 17 years of experience in web development, I’ve worked across industries including education, utilities, and nuclear energy. My work often involves PHP, MySQL, and full LAMP stack development—along with database design, optimization, and creating applications that solve real-world problems.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me outdoors. I’m an avid backpacker, hiker, and nature photographer. I run my own photography business in Russellville, AR, specializing in capturing the beauty of the outdoors and helping families preserve their memories. I also volunteer with the Boy Scouts of America, leading high-adventure trips, planning events, and helping the next generation develop a love for the outdoors.

A few things I’m currently working on:

Developing applications that help scouts track merit badges and achievements.

Planning adventures with my family and checking items off our bucket list.

Exploring new ways AI and data analytics can improve workflows and decision-making.

At the core, I’m driven by curiosity—whether it’s learning a new technology, exploring a trail I haven’t hiked, or capturing a landscape through my lens. This site is where I share my work, projects, and adventures.
